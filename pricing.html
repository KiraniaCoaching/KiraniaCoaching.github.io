About My Coaching

Find me at kirania1 on Discord and Contact me once you've booked a session, or if you have any questions!

For best convenience I organise all my sessions on Discord and the sessions are all done in voice calls preferably through Discord. Most often my students will screenshare their game in the voice call and I give advice over their gameplay. I have found this to be the most effective coaching method but I am willing to spend the sessions with me screensharing my game, playing 1v1s or in others ways that players are interested in.

If you are interested in longer packages then the ones offered (for a slightly cheaper price) that can be coordinated, just contact me and ask! And every player can tailor their coaching experience to whatever they feel they'd like they want most help with, whether it be Moving, No Move, NMPZ or focusing on learning all the clues for regionguessing particular regions or countries.
